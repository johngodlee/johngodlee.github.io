Fresh pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Mix ingredients into a shaggy ball
  2. Knead for 8-10 minutes until a smooth dough
  3. Wrap in clingfilm then rest in the fridge for at least 30 minutes
  4. Roll and cut into desired shapes
  5. Boil in salted water for 3 minutes or until done
