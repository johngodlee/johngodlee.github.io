Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove - minced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a jar. Seal the jar then shake vigorously to combine
