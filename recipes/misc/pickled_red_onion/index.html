Quick pickled red onions

Ingredients

  • 1 large red onion
  • 175 ml apple cider vinegar
  • 60 ml water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 0.5 tsp chilli flakes

Method

  • Heat the vinegar, water, salt, brown sugar, mustard seeds, bay leaves and chilli flakes in a saucepan until boiling, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Finely slice the red onion and transfer to a glass container.
  • Poor the liquid over the onions and mix thoroughly.
  • Leave until cooled, then seal container and refrigerate.
