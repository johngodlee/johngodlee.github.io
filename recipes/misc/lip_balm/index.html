Lip Balm

Ingredients

  • Shea butter 12.5g
  • Beeswax 7.5g
  • Cocoa butter 5g
  • Almond and Calendula oil 15ml

Method

  1. Melt shea butter, beeswax, cocoa butter in metal bowl over boiling pan
  2. When melted, add almond and calendula oil
  3. Mix well
  4. Pour into tins while hot
