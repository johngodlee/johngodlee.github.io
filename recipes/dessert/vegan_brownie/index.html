Vegan brownies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 0.75 cup cocoa powder
  • 0.5 cup vegetable oil
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 cup nuts (optional)
  • 0.5 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Heat water and 1 cup of flour over low heat in a pan, until gooey
  3. Cool completely
  4. Mix sugar, salt, vanilla, cocoa powder, vegetable oil, add to flour mixture
  5. Mix well
  6. Add the remaining 1 cup flour, baking powder, nuts, chocolate chips, mix thoroughly
  7. Grease baking pan
  8. Bake for 25 minutes or until middle is cooked
