Microwave sponge pudding

Ingredients

  • 100 g self-raising flour
  • 100 g butter
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 2 eggs

Lemon variation

  • Zest half lemon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp lemon curd

Chocolate variation

  • 25 g cocoa powder (remove 25 g self-raising flour)
  • 8 squares dark chocolate

Method

  1. Beat together flour, sugar, butter and eggs in a bowl
  2. Mix in cocoa powder for chocolate version, or lemon zest and vanilla extract for lemon version
  3. In a second bowl, dollop lemon curd or chocolate
  4. Transfer mixture into second bowl
  5. Microwave for 3 minutes, until fluffy. Rest for a minute, then microwave for another 90 seconds
