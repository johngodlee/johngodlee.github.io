Turkey or chicken burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (454 g) chicken or turkey mince
  • 0.5 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 0.25 brown onions
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 0.5 tsp paprika
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients (except olive oil) in a large bowl. Do not over-mix
  2. Form into 5-7 patties. Press down the middle of each patty to maintain their shape while cooking
  3. Pre-heat a large frying pan with the olive oil
  4. Add the burger patties and cook over medium heat
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides
