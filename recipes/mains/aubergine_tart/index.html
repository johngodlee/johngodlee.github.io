Aubergine tart

Ingredients

  • 320 g pre-rolled puff pastry
  • 2 aubergines
  • 200 g feta
  • 1 egg
  • 100 ml olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp harissa paste
  • Mixed herbs
  • 2 garlic cloves

Alternative toppings

  • Beef tomatoes
  • Courgette
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 200C
  2. Thinly slice the aubergine
  3. Brush the aubergine with oil on both sides
  4. Fry the aubergine on medium-high heat until tender and charred
  5. Grease and line a large baking sheet
  6. Unroll the pastry and lay on the lined baking sheet. Score around the edge to create a 2 cm border
  7. Mince the garlic
  8. Mix the tomato paste, harissa paste, garlic and herbs in a bowl, then smear over the pastry base
  9. Sprinkle feta over the pastry
  10. Arrange the aubergine slices over the pastry base, slightly overlapping
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the pastry is golden brown
  12. Leave to stand for 10 minutes before serving
