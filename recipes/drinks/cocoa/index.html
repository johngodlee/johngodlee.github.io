Cocoa

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tsp milk powder

Method

  1. Add the milk powder and cocoa powder to a mug
  2. Boil water enough to fill the mug
  3. Add a small amount of the water to the mug and mix thoroughly to create a paste
  4. Add the rest of the water
  5. Add the brown sugar and stir until dissolved
