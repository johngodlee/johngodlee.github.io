Freezer breakfast burritos

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 10 wheat tortillas
  • 1 435 g tin refried beans
  • 250 g mushrooms
  • 30 g fresh coriander
  • 120 g mild cheddar cheese
  • 1 green chilli
  • 5 spring onions
  • Hot sauce
  • Salsa
  • Butter or vegetable oil
  • Greaseproof paper

Optionally:

  • Spinach
  • Fried bell peppers
  • Cooked green lentils
  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Queso fresco

Method

  1. Finely dice the mushrooms
  2. In a large frying pan, fry the mushrooms in butter or vegetable oil until most of the water has burned off, then decant into a bowl
  3. Beat the eggs and scramble in the pan until quite dry, leave to cool
  4. Finely chop the coriander, grate the cheese, de-seed and finely slice the green chilli, finely slice the spring onions, decant the refried beans into a bowl
  5. Smear the centre of a tortilla with refried beans, then add eggs, mushrooms, cheese, green chilli, spring onions, coriander, salsa and hot sauce
  6. Roll up the tortilla then wrap in greaseproof paper and freeze
  • To reheat, microwave at 800w for ~4 minutes
