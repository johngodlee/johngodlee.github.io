Porridge

To make one serving.

Ingredients

  • 50 g porridge oats
  • 20 g milk powder
  • 200 ml water

Method

  1. In a saucepan, mix oats and milk powder
  2. Add water and mix thoroughly
  3. Heat to boiling, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring to avoid oats sticking to bottom of pan
  4. Serve with:
    • Peanut butter
    • Raisins
    • Honey
    • Muesli
    • Nuts
    • Cocoa powder
    • CInnamon and brown sugar
