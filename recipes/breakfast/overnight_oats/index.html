Overnight oats

To make one serving.

Ingredients

  • 50 g porridge oats
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 100 ml milk, or equivalent milk powder and water
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 50 g mixed berries
  • 0.5 tbsp nut butter

Extras

  • Chia seeds
  • Vanilla
  • Cream
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Chopped nuts
  • Dried fruit
  • Fresh berries
  • Sliced apple
  • Sliced banana
  • Jam
  • Lemon zest
  • Chocolate chips

Method

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix thoroughly
  3. Keep overnight in the fridge in a container with a lid
