Web-app for library of GPX files

2026-04-04

I got Claude to help me write a web-app to display a directory of GPX files on a leaflet map , with meta-data like tags and notes, and the option to download a selected GPX file. I wanted to do this to keep a public archive of the cycling and walking routes I like to do, so that others can use those routes as well.
